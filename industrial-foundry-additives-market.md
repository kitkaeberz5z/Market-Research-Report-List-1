<p><p>The Industrial Foundry Additives Market consists of three primary types: organic, inorganic, and hybrid additives. Organic additives are derived from carbon-based compounds, enhancing the properties of foundry materials, such as improving mold strength. Inorganic additives, typically minerals or metals, provide superior thermal stability and resistance to chemical reactions. Hybrid additives combine both organic and inorganic components, offering a balanced approach to performance and efficiency. Each type is crucial in optimizing casting processes and improving the quality of finished products.</p></p>
