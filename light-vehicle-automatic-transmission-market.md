<p><p>The light vehicle automatic transmission market is segmented into four main types: </p><p>1. **AMT (Automated Manual Transmission)**: Combines manual transmission with an automated system for gear shifts, offering better fuel efficiency.</p><p>2. **AT (Automatic Transmission)**: Uses a hydraulic system for seamless gear shifts, delivering a smooth driving experience.</p><p>3. **CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)**: Provides an infinite range of gear ratios for optimal performance and fuel efficiency.</p><p>4. **DCT (Dual-Clutch Transmission)**: Employs two clutches for quicker gear changes, enhancing acceleration and performance. </p><p>Each type caters to different driving preferences and efficiency needs.</p></p>
