<p><p>A two-port solenoid valve is an electromechanical device that controls fluid flow through a pipeline by using an electromagnet to open or close the valve. These valves are commonly utilized in various applications, including industrial automation, HVAC systems, water management, and various process control systems. Their design typically features two ports: one for the fluid inlet and another for the outlet, which allows for efficient flow regulation.</p><p>The two-port solenoid valve market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.5% during the forecast period.
