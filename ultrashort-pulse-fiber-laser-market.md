<p><p>The ultrashort pulse fiber laser market is primarily categorized into two types: femtosecond lasers and picosecond lasers. Femtosecond lasers emit pulses with durations in the range of 10^-15 seconds and are ideal for high-precision applications like micromachining and medical procedures, due to their ability to process materials without thermal damage. In contrast, picosecond lasers, with pulse durations around 10^-12 seconds, are commonly used for applications such as laser marking and engraving, providing efficient energy transfer and precision control.</p></p>
