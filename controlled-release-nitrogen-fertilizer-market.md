<p><p>The Controlled-Release Nitrogen Fertilizer market is categorized into four main types: Nitrate Form, Ammonia Form, Ammonium Form, and Urea Form. Nitrate Form releases nitrogen quickly, benefiting plants needing immediate nutrients. Ammonia Form provides nitrogen in a stable form, suitable for slow absorption. Ammonium Form combines quick and slow release, catering to various plant needs. Urea Form is versatile and widely used, offering a gradual release of nitrogen, enhancing efficiency and minimizing environmental impact. Each type serves specific agricultural requirements.</p></p>
