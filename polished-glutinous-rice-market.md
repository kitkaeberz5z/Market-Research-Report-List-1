<p><p>The polished glutinous rice market features two primary types: long sticky rice and round sticky rice. Long sticky rice, often used in savory dishes, is characterized by its elongated grains and chewy texture, making it ideal for meals like sushi and rice cakes. Both types cater to diverse culinary preferences, driving varied demand in the global market.</p></p>
