<p><p>A Semi Automated Coagulation Analyzer is a medical device used to assess blood coagulation by measuring the time it takes for blood to clot. This analyzer provides a reliable and efficient means of monitoring coagulation parameters, which are critical for diagnosing various bleeding disorders and managing anticoagulant therapy. The semi-automated nature of these analyzers allows for reduced manual intervention, minimizing errors and increasing productivity in clinical laboratories.</p><p>The Semi Automated Coagulation Analyzer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.8% during the forecast period.
