<p><p>The corded endodontic electric motor system market is segmented into two main types: without integrated apex locator and with integrated apex locator. Systems without an integrated apex locator focus solely on providing continuous rotational torque for endodontic procedures, allowing for precise canal shaping. In contrast, systems with integrated apex locators enhance functionality by combining canal measurement with rotary motion, enabling dentists to determine the working length accurately. Both types are designed to improve efficiency and outcomes in root canal treatments.</p></p>
