<p><ul><li>"Convection Ovens"</li><li>"Vapour Phase Ovens"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surface Mount Technology (SMT) reflow oven market includes two primary types: convection ovens and vapor phase ovens. Convection ovens utilize forced hot air to evenly heat the circuit board, ensuring consistent soldering across components. They are widely used due to their versatility and efficiency. Vapor phase ovens, on the other hand, operate by immersing the board in a vapor-infused fluid, providing precise thermal control and minimizing thermal shock. Both types play crucial roles in modern electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
