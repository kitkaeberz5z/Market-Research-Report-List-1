<p><p>Dextran is a complex polysaccharide derived from the fermentation of sucrose by specific bacteria, commonly Leuconostoc species. It is widely used in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food, and cosmetics, owing to its biocompatibility, thickening, and stabilizing properties. In the pharmaceutical sector, dextran serves as a volume expander in medical applications and as an excipient in drug formulations.</p><p>The Dextran Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period, fueled by increasing demand in healthcare and pharmaceutical applications. The rise in surgical procedures and the necessity for blood volume expanders are propelling market growth. Furthermore, the expanding food and beverage sector, with dextran's use as a food additive and stabilizer, also contributes to market expansion.</p><p>Recent trends indicate a growing focus on natural and sustainable products, leading to an increased interest in dextran as a versatile and eco-friendly ingredient. Innovations in dextran's applications, such as its potential in nanomedicine and targeted drug delivery systems, are also shaping the market.
