<p><p>The ultrasound probe market consists of various types tailored for specific medical applications. Linear type probes are used for superficial structures, providing high-resolution images. Convex type probes offer a wider field view, suitable for abdominal scans. Phased array probes excel in cardiology, delivering images in confined spaces. Endocavitary probes are designed for internal examinations, such as transvaginal or transrectal imaging. Other market types may include specialized probes for veterinary use or pediatric applications, enhancing diagnostic capabilities across diverse medical fields.</p></p>
