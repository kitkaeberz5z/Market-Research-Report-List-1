<p><p>The airway management tubes market comprises various types, primarily including endotracheal tubes and tracheostomy tubes. Endotracheal tubes are inserted through the mouth or nose into the trachea to maintain airway patency during anesthesia or emergency situations. Tracheostomy tubes, on the other hand, are surgically inserted through the neck into the trachea, facilitating long-term ventilation or airway access in patients with obstructive conditions. Other market segments may include specialty tubes designed for specific surgical or patient needs, enhancing airway management versatility.</p></p>
