<p><ul><li>Absorbent Type</li><li>Reflective Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IR-cut glass market features two primary types: absorbent and reflective. The absorbent type utilizes materials that effectively absorb infrared radiation, preventing it from passing through and ensuring clear visibility by blocking heat. In contrast, the reflective type employs coatings that reflect infrared light while allowing visible light to transmit, enhancing energy efficiency. Both types are essential in applications like cameras, optical devices, and energy-efficient buildings, balancing visibility and thermal management for optimal performance.</p></p>
