<p><p>The flat-field objective market is primarily divided into two types: fused-silica and optical-glass objectives. Fused-silica objectives are made from high-purity silica, offering excellent transmission and thermal stability, making them ideal for high-performance applications like spectroscopy and laser systems. Optical-glass objectives, on the other hand, utilize various glass materials for versatile applications, providing good optical quality at a more economical price point. Both types are crucial in fields such as microscopy, imaging, and metrology, catering to diverse user needs.</p></p>
