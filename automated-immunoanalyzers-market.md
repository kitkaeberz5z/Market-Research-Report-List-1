<p><p>Automated immunoanalyzers are categorized into semi-automated and fully automated systems. Semi-automated analyzers require some manual intervention, such as sample preparation and loading, making them suitable for smaller laboratories with limited throughput. Fully automated immunoanalyzers, on the other hand, handle the entire testing process from sample preparation to result reporting without human intervention, optimizing efficiency and throughput. This allows larger facilities to manage higher volumes of tests with reduced human error, increasing overall diagnostic reliability and speed.</p></p>
