<p><p>Employer of Record (EOR) services operate primarily through two market models: the Aggregator Model and the Wholly Owned Infrastructure Model. The Aggregator Model connects companies with existing EOR services, consolidating resources and expanding service reach without heavy investment. In contrast, the Wholly Owned Infrastructure Model involves companies establishing their own EOR entity, providing complete control over operations, compliance, and employee management. Both models allow businesses to manage workforce complexities while ensuring regulatory adherence and streamlining HR processes.</p></p>
