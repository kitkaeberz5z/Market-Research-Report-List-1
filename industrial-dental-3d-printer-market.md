<p><strong>The Industrial Dental 3D Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stereolithography (SLA)</li><li>Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM)</li><li>Digital Light Processing (DLP)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial dental 3D printer market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Stereolithography (SLA)** utilizes a laser to cure liquid resin layer by layer, producing highly detailed dental models. </p><p>2. **Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM)** extrudes thermoplastic filament to build dental devices, favored for its cost-effectiveness. </p><p>3. **Digital Light Processing (DLP)** employs a projector to polymerize resin quickly, enhancing production speed with precision. </p><p>4. **Other** methods include binder jetting and selective laser sintering, catering to specific dental applications and materials.</p></p>
